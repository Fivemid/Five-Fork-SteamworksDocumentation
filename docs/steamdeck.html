Steam Deck

Steam Deck is a next generation handheld gaming device - letting players take their Steam library with them on the couch or on the go. Steam Deck includes a high resolution display, full-size ergonomic controls, updated Steam UI, and enough horsepower to run current-gen games.

This site is a resource for Steam developers - here you can learn about developing for Steam Deck, developer kits, and more.
