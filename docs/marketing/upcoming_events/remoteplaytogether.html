Remote Play Together 2022



The Steam Remote Play Together Sale is all about shopping for great games and playing your favorites with your friends. This year's event runs February 28 - March 7, 2022 starting at 10am Pacific.

Eligibility

Any game that supports the Remote Play Together feature, and has enabled the "Remote Play Together" icon on the game's store page, is eligible to participate. For more details on Remote Play, please see Remote Play Documentation

How to participate

To participate with your game, simply visit "Apps & Packages -> Sale Approvals" and enter a discount for your eligible games. Here is a direct link to that discounting dashboard: https://partner.steamgames.com/promotion/discounts/dashboard/

Asset Kit

Here you can download a kit of images and templates to use in your own social media channels when telling your fans about your participation in the Remote Play Together Sale. These images are designed to fit well with various social media channels and you are welcome to use these assets any time you are ready to let players know that you'll be part of the event.
