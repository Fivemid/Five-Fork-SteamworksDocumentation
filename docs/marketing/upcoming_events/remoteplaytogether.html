Remote Play Together 2022



The Steam Remote Play Together Sale is all about shopping for great games and playing your favorites with your friends. This year's event runs February 28 - March 7, 2022 starting at 10am Pacific.

Eligibility

Any game that supports the Remote Play Together feature, and has enabled the "Remote Play Together" feature on the game's store page, is eligible to participate.

Adding Remote Play Together Feature Support
If your title has local multiplayer support, it may be a good candidate for the Remote Play Together feature. The feature enables a player to invite a friend to join their game session via Remote Play streaming, even when their friend doesnâ€™t own the game, or even have a Steam account, as though they invited their friend over to their house. Remote Play Together can be a great way to introduce new players to your title.

The feature is enabled automatically for games that are indicated to have Local Multiplayer, Local Co-op, or Shared/Split Screen capability, but you can enable or disable it yourself to match whether this feature is appropriate for your game.

To enable the feature, edit your title's settings in the Steamworks Store Page Admin. On the Basic Info tab, under Supported Features, check Remote Play Together.

For more details, please see the Remote Play Documentation.

Discounts

All titles with the Remote Play Together feature enabled have been invited to participate in the discount event.

If you have recently enabled the feature or released your game with support for Remote Play Together, contact Steam Support to request an invitation to the participate in the discount event.

Once invited, visit your discounting dashboard (Apps & Packages, Sale Approvals) and then enter any discounts.

Using the Steamworks Promotions dashboard, enter your discounts any time before the sale begins, on Feb 28th at 10am Pacific.

Livestreams

To participate in the event without offering a discount, simply host a livestream event featuring your title with Remote Play Together support anytime throughout the event, 2/28-3/7 2022.

Event Visibility

This is an official Steam Themed Sale and will be promoted significantly on the homepage of Steam and via our social media channels. The event will highlight games with discounts and will have a broadcast showcasing games that are running livestreams, all at https://store.steampowered.com/remoteplay_together.
