Remote Play Together 2022



The Steam Remote Play Together Sale is all about shopping for great games and playing your favorites with your friends. This year's event runs February 28 - March 7, 2022 starting at 10am Pacific.

Eligibility

Any game that supports the Remote Play Together feature is eligible to participate. For more details on Remote Play, please see Remote Play Documentation

How to participate

To participate with your game, simply visit "Apps & Packages -> Sale Approvals" and enter a discount for your eligible games.
