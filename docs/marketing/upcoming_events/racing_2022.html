Steam Racing Fest (2022)

Steam Racing Fest is a Festival of Fleetness celebrating things that go fast competitively and features games with the primary mechanic of racing, whether on wheels, water, or in the air.

This event runs May 23 at 10:00 AM PDT (GMT-7) to May 30 at 10:00 AM PDT (GMT-7) and will include discounting, as well as sections for unreleased games and games with demos.

Eligibility

This event is targeted toward games that are primarily thought of as one of these types of games:
  • Racing
  • Combat Racing


Participating games must have racing as the primary game mechanic. Examples of games that may not include racing as the primary game mechanic and thus may not be eligible include:

  • Racing Management / Business
  • Driving / Flying / Running / Sailing / etc.
  • Vehicle Simulation
  • Combat Racing that's mostly combat
  • Parkour
  • Platformers not built directly around a time trial component
  • Multi-game games that do not feature racing for the majority of gameplay

Invitation Process

We'll be inviting specific games to participate in Steam Racing Fest based on the criteria above. This invitation will go out to Steamworks users who have set "Receive Steamworks Communication" permission for eligible games.

The invitation will ask you to register that game for Steam Racing Fest, which is as easy as clicking one checkbox and saving. That registration will tell us which games intend to participate.

Once registered, users in your account with "Edit Pricing Permissions" will then see the Steam Racing Fest sale appear in the new discounting approval tools and you can enter discounts for your game.

If you have not received an email invitation and believe that you have a game that fits the criteria described above, please check your list of eligible games. If your game is not present there and you believe it meets the above criteria, please Contact us here.

Ways To Participate

Discounts

A discount is not required to participate in Steam Racing Fest, though the Steam Racing Fest page will prominently feature discounted games. All invited titles can feature a discount unless you already have another discount scheduled too soon prior or following Steam Racing Fest.

Reminder: As of March 28th, all sales and discount opportunities are subject to the same 28-day cooldown between discounts. The only exceptions are the four major Steam seasonal sales. For more details on the discounting rule changes, please see Discounting Rule Changes.

Once invited, visit your discounting dashboard (Apps & Packages -> Sale Approvals) to see your invited games and to enter any discounts.

Enter your discounts any time before the sale begins on May 23 at 10:00 AM PDT.

Demos

If you've got an upcoming game that fits the criteria above (or even if you have a released game) you may release a demo to include in the fest. We'll have a section for games with free demos, as well as lists of upcoming releases.

Steam Racing Fest Visibility

This is an official Steam Themed Sale and will be promoted significantly on the homepage of Steam and via our social media channels. The event will highlight games with discounts and will have sections for upcoming games and games with playable demos.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Do I need to register my game before entering a discount?
A. Yes, you need to do the following things in order:
  1. Follow the link in the invitation email, or click this link to see your eligible games and register each: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/sale_racing_2022
  2. Once a game is registered, you can then enter discounts on the new Sale Approvals page here: https://partner.steamgames.com/promotion/discounts/dashboard/

Q. Will there be livestreaming as part of this event?
A. Livestreaming will not be featured on the sale page, but you are welcome to livestream on your own game's store page(s) as you wish.
