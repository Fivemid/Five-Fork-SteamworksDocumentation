2021 Winter Sale



The Steam Winter Sale is all about shopping for great games and voting for your favorites in the 2021 Steam Awards. This year's event runs Dec 22, 2021 - Jan 5th, 2022 at 10am Pacific.

Marketing Assets

Visit Dropbox for asset templates you can customize to promote your participation in the sale, and to solicit specific Steam Awards votes from your fans.

Steam Awards Voting

Congratulations to this year's nominees! To solicit your fans' votes in the Steam Awards, use the relevant piece of artwork available on Dropbox along with your title's box art when communicating with players and fans on Steam and social media.
