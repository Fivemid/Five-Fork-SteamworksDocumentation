Steam Survival Fest (2022)



Steam Survival Fest is a Festival of Fortitude celebrating the struggle to beat the elements with discounts and demos on current and upcoming survival games of all kinds.

This event runs August 1 at 10:00 AM PDT (GMT-7) to August 8 at 10:00 AM PDT (GMT-7) and will include discounting, as well as sections for unreleased games and games with demos.

Eligibility

This event is targeted toward games that are primarily thought of as one of these types of games:
  • Survival
  • Survival Horror
  • Open World Survival Craft

Participating games must have survival as the primary game mechanic. Survival games focus on using defensive tactics to stay in the game—against destructive forces including nature or supernatural beings—and typically include:

  • Defensive gameplay focused against an environment or a monster/monsters
  • Some adversity the player must overcome
  • Resource and health management (e.g. hunger and thirst)
  • The player trying to survive vs. attacking others who are trying to survive
  • Colony sim / building games with survival elements such as NPCs that attack or environmental damage the player needs to shelter from

Examples of games that may not include survival (as described above) as the primary game mechanic and thus may not be eligible include:

  • Games in which the primary aim is to inflict harm on others, including battle royales and MOBAs
  • Multiplayer games with no specific survival component
  • MMOs with no specific survival component
  • Straight horror games
  • Pure Tower Defense games (though some survival games do have tower defense mechanics)

Invitation Process

We'll be inviting specific games to participate in Steam Survival Fest based on the criteria above. This invitation will go out to Steamworks users who have set "Receive Steamworks Communication" permission for eligible games.

The invitation will ask you to register that game for Steam Survival Fest, which is as easy as clicking one checkbox and saving. That registration will tell us which games intend to participate.

Once registered, users in your account with "Edit Pricing Permissions" will then see the Steam Survival Fest sale appear in the new discounting approval tools and you can enter discounts for your game.

If you have not received an email invitation and believe that you have a game that fits the criteria described above, please check your list of eligible games. If your game is not present there and you believe it meets the above criteria, please contact us here.

Ways To Participate

Discounts

A discount is not required to participate in Steam Survival Fest, though the Steam Survival Fest page will prominently feature discounted games. All invited titles can feature a discount unless you already have another discount scheduled too soon prior or following Steam Survival Fest.

Reminder: As of March 28, 2022, all sales and discount opportunities are subject to the same 28-day cooldown between discounts. The only exceptions are the four major Steam seasonal sales. For more details on the discounting rule changes, please see Discounting Rule Changes.

Once invited, visit your discounting dashboard (Apps & Packages -> Sale Approvals) to see your invited games and to enter any discounts.

Enter your discounts any time before the sale begins on August 1 at 10:00 AM PDT.

Demos

If you've got an upcoming game that fits the criteria above (or even if you have a released game) you may release a demo to include in the fest. We'll have a section for games with free demos, as well as lists of upcoming releases.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Do I need to register my game before entering a discount?
A. Yes, you need to do the following things in order:
  1. Follow the link in the invitation email, or click this link to see your eligible games and register each: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/survival_2022
  2. Once a game is registered, you can then enter discounts on the new Sale Approvals page here: https://partner.steamgames.com/promotion/discounts/dashboard/

Q. Will there be livestreaming as part of this event?
A. Livestreaming will not be featured on the sale page, but you are welcome to livestream on your own game's store page as you wish.
