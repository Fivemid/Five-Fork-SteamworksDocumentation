Steam JRPG Sale, 2022

The Steam JRPG sale is a celebration of games built around the JRPG mechanics and style.

This even runs March 14 at 10am Pacific - March 21st at 10am Pacific.

Eligibility

The Steam JRPG Sale is open to games built around the JRPG style and mechanics of gameplay.

Invitation Process

We'll be inviting specific games to participate in the Steam JRPG Sale that have the JRPG tag applied to their store page. This invitation will go out to the Steamworks users that have "Can Edit Pricing and Discounts" permission for eligible games.

If you have not received an email invitation and believe that you have a game that fits the criteria described above, please Contact us here.

Discounts

A discount is not required to participate in the Steam JRPG sale, though we will be prioritizing the visibility and showcasing of games that have a discount during the event. All invited titles can feature a discount, and is exempt from discount cooldown rules.

Reminder: As of March 28th, all sales and discount opportunities are subject to the same 28-day cooldown between discounts. The only exceptions are the four major Steam seasonal sales. For more details on the discounting rule changes, please see Discounting Rule Changes

Once invited, visit your discounting dashboard (Apps & Packages -> Sale Approvals) to see your invited games and to enter any discounts.

Enter your discounts any time before the sale begins, on March 14th at 10am Pacific.

Event Visibility

This is an official Steam Themed Sale and will be promoted significantly on the homepage of Steam and via our social media channels. The event will highlight games with discounts.
