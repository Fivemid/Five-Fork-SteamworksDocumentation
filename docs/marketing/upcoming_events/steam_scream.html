Steam Scream: The Revenge (Halloween)



Steam Scream Fest celebrates the spookiest season of the year with discounts, demos, and upcoming releases.

This event runs October 26 at 10:00 AM PDT (UTC-7) to November 2 at 10:00 AM PDT (UTC-7) and will include eligible titles with sections for discounted titles, unreleased titles in Coming Soon mode, games with demos, and more.


For general information about Steam themed sale events, including details on the selection and invitation process, ways to participate (discounts, demos, and upcoming releases), event visibility and promotion, in-event game visibility, and marketing materials permissions, please visit the Steam Themed Sale Events parent documentation page.

Important Dates

  • October 19 - The trailer announcing the event launches on this day. At this point, you're welcome to share your participation in the event. Assets for this purpose will be available in the Asset Kit below.
  • October 26, 10am PDT - The event starts. This is the deadline for entering a discount, and opting out if you no longer want to participate.

Eligibility

Games must be launched or in Coming Soon mode to be eligible for this event. If your game is not yet in Coming Soon, you can appeal to have your game join once you've released your Store Page. The base game must be eligible for the event; DLCs alone are not eligible.

This event is targeted toward games primarily designed around spooky and festive themes. Appropriate games would be centered around these themes vs. merely including one character or cosmetic.

Relevant tags include:
  • Horror 
  • Survival Horror 
  • Vampires
  • Zombies 
  • Werewolves
  • Supernatural
  • Dark Fantasy
  • Lovecraftian

In-Game Events

Steam will not be surfacing spooky in-game events as part of Steam Scream: The Revenge. We’ve received a significant amount of feedback from past Halloween sales that, due to the way in-game events (with wildly varying degrees of spookiness) are surfaced on the sale page, we can offer a better developer and player experience by focusing exclusively on inherently spooky games themselves.

So, to make it easier for developers and Steam users to connect over the scary games Steam Scream is intended to celebrate, Steam will only be surfacing spooky games during the sale, and not in-game spooky events. That being said, you’re still more than welcome to do spooky in-game events and market them to your audience however you see fit.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. I haven't received an email invitation and have a game I believe fits the eligibility criteria described above. How can I confirm whether my game has been invited and request an invitation if it has not been invited?
A. If you haven't received an email invitation and believe you have a game that fits the eligibility criteria described above, please check:

  • Your list of eligible games here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/halloween_2023
  • The registration page for your game by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/halloween_2023/<appid>
  • Your base game's App Admin page (in the Marketing Tools & Data section) for the opt-in by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/apps/landing/<appID>

If your game is not found via the paths above and you believe it meets the eligibility criteria, please use the appeals process available at the bottom of the registration page.

If your game was found via the paths above but you didn't receive an email, please check your Steamworks communication preferences (instructions available in the Steam Themed Sale Events parent documentation page).

Q. Do I need to register my game before entering a discount?
A. Yes, you need to do the following things in order:
  1. Follow the link in the invitation email, or click this link to see your eligible games and register each: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/halloween_2023
  2. Once a game is registered, you can then enter discounts on the Discount Management page: https://partner.steamgames.com/promotion/discounts/dashboard/

Q. Will there be livestreaming as part of this event?
A. Livestreaming will not be featured on the sale page, but you're welcome to livestream on your own game's store page as you wish.

Q. I've changed my plans. How do I cancel my registration?
A. Just return to the registration page for your game by inserting your appID at the end of this URL https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/halloween_2023/<appid> and deselect the checkbox at the very bottom of the page to remove your game from the event.
