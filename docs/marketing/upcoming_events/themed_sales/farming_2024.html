Steam Farming Fest 2024



Steam Farming Fest celebrates—you guessed it—farming games! Is your game about farming? Can your character actually farm, or make minions farm for them? Then congratulations! This is the event for you.

This event runs April 29 at 10:00 AM PDT (UTC-7) to May 6 at 10:00 AM PDT (UTC-7) and will include eligible titles with sections for discounted titles, unreleased titles in Coming Soon mode, games with demos, and more.


For general information about Steam themed sale events, including details on the selection and invitation process, ways to participate (discounts, demos, and upcoming releases), event visibility and promotion, in-event game visibility, and marketing materials permissions, please visit the Steam Themed Sale Events parent documentation page.

Important Dates

  • April 25 - The trailer announcing the event launches on this day. At this point, you're welcome to share your participation in the event. Assets for this purpose will be available in the Asset Kit below.
  • April 29, 10am PDT - The event starts. This is the deadline for entering a discount, and opting out if you no longer want to participate.

Eligibility

Games must be launched or in Coming Soon mode to be eligible for these events. If your game is not yet in Coming Soon, you can appeal to have your game join once you've released your Store Page. The base game must be eligible for the event; DLCs alone are not eligible. Eligibility is determined by the tags applied to your game's store page, as well as the gameplay that is evident from how you describe and represent your game on your store page.

If your game is primarily about farming, making the character farm, or making minions to farm for you, then it is eligible for this event.

Does your character only farm part of the time? You might still be eligible, as long as farming is a core part of your gameplay and not just a side thing or mini-game.

Now, we're not picky about what you're farming—it could be turnips, rice, star shards, fish, or magic potions—but you do have to be growing something. You have to be able to harvest those things for money or gameplay progression to be eligible for this event. You should probably also have some tilled earth at some point, a few seed/sprout/crop images, and maybe a watering can?

Games likely to be eligible will (at minimum) include one or more of these tags:
  • Farming
  • Farming Sim
  • Agriculture

Games likely to be eligible typically feature mechanics and/or elements including:
  • You must be able to grow something (fish, vegetables, fruit) that can be harvested for money or gameplay progression
  • Games where the primary game loop does not involve the above are not eligible

Examples of games that may not include farming (as described above) as the primary game mechanic and thus may not be eligible include:
  • Games with farming DLC
  • Games where the primary gameplay is economic
  • Games where the farm is a backdrop

Check your list of eligible games here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/farming

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. I haven't received an email invitation and have a game I believe fits the eligibility criteria described above. How can I confirm whether my game has been invited and request an invitation if it has not been invited?
A. If you haven't received an email invitation and believe you have a game that fits the eligibility criteria described above, please check:

  • Your list of eligible games here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/farming
  • The registration page for your game by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/farming/<appid>
  • Your base game's App Admin page (in the Marketing Tools & Data section) for the opt-in by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/apps/landing/<appID>

If your game is not found via the paths above and you believe it meets the eligibility criteria, please use the appeals process available at the bottom of the registration page.

If your game was found via the paths above but you didn't receive an email, please check your Steamworks communication preferences (instructions available in the Steam Themed Sale Events parent documentation page).

Q. Do I need to register my game before entering a discount?
A. Yes, you need to do the following things in order:
  1. Follow the link in the invitation email, or click this link to see your eligible games and register each: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/farming
  2. Once a game is registered, you can then enter discounts on the Discount Management page: https://partner.steamgames.com/promotion/discounts/dashboard/

Q. Will there be livestreaming as part of this event?
A. Livestreaming will not be featured on the sale page, but you're welcome to livestream on your own game's store page as you wish.

Q. I've changed my plans. How do I cancel my registration?
A. Just return to the registration page for your game by inserting your appID at the end of this URL https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/farming/<appid> and deselect the checkbox at the very bottom of the page to remove your game from the event.
