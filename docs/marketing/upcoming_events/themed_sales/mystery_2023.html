Steam Mystery Fest (2023)



Steam Mystery Fest celebrates the spirit of investigation and solving mysteries—both big and small—with discounts and demos on current and upcoming mystery and detective games of all kinds.

This event runs February 20 at 10:00 AM PST (UTC-8) to February 27 at 10:00 AM PST (UTC-8) and will include discounting, as well as sections for unreleased games and games with demos.



For general information about Steam themed sale events, including detail on the selection and invitation process, ways to participate (discounts, demos, and upcoming releases), event visibility and promotion, in-event game visibility, and marketing materials permissions, please visit the Steam Themed Sale Events parent documentation page.

Important Dates

  • January 9 - Deadline to register for the event and opt-in to marketing materials permissions (if additional opt-in has been requested by Valve), enter a discount, and ensure you have at least one trailer set to public.
  • February 20, 10am PST - Deadline to register for the event and enter a discount if you would like to participate with one, or opt out if you registered and your plans have changed.

Eligibility

This event is targeted toward games that are primarily thought of as one of these types of games:

  • Mystery
  • Detective
  • Social Deduction

Participating games must have mystery solving and/or investigation as the primary game mechanic and typically feature additional mechanics and/or elements including:

  • Noir

Examples of games that may not include mystery solving and/or investigation (as described above) as the primary game mechanic and thus may not be eligible include:

  • Combat-forward games, incl. superhero themes
  • Spy games
  • Straight puzzle games, incl. hidden object games, with no detective work / investigation
  • Escape room games with escape rooms as the only mechanic
  • Horror games that may include a mystery element but that do not focus on investigation
  • Walking simulators without a primary focus on solving mysteries

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. I haven't received an email invitation and have a game I believe fits the eligibility criteria described above. How can I confirm whether my game has been invited and request an invitation if it has not been invited?
A. If you haven't received an email invitation and believe you have a game that fits the eligibility criteria described above, please check:

  • Your list of eligible games here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/mystery_detective_2023
  • The registration page for your game by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/mystery_detective_2023/<appid>
  • Your base game's App Admin page (in the Marketing Tools & Data section) for the opt-in by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/apps/landing/<appID>

If your game is not found via the paths above and you believe it meets the eligibility criteria, please contact us here.

If your game was found via the paths above but you didn't receive an email, please check your Steamworks communication preferences (instructions available in the Steam Themed Sale Events parent documentation page).

Q. Do I need to register my game before entering a discount?
A. Yes, you need to do the following things in order:
  1. Follow the link in the invitation email, or click this link to see your eligible games and register each: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/mystery_detective_2023
  2. Once a game is registered, you can then enter discounts on the Sale Approvals page: https://partner.steamgames.com/promotion/discounts/dashboard/

Q. Will there be livestreaming as part of this event?
A. Livestreaming will not be featured on the sale page, but you're welcome to livestream on your own game's store page as you wish.

Q. I've changed my plans. How do I cancel my registration?
A. Just return to the registration page for your game by inserting your appID at the end of this URL https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/mystery_detective_2023/<appid> and deselect the checkbox at the very bottom of the page to remove your game from the event.
