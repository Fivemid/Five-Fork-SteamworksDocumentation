Steam Deckbuilders Fest (2024)



Steam Deckbuilders Fest celebrates card games where you can build and manage your deck of cards. It doesn't necessarily have to be represented with cards, but the mechanic should be easily understood as a deckbuilding kind of game. Those cards can be your typical attack/defense/special effect cards, but they can also represent resources, characters, or other non-combat related things.

This event runs March 25 at 10:00 AM PDT (UTC-7) to April 1 at 10:00 AM PDT (UTC-7) and will include eligible titles with sections for discounted titles, unreleased titles in Coming Soon mode, games with demos, and more.


For general information about Steam themed sale events, including details on the selection and invitation process, ways to participate (discounts, demos, and upcoming releases), event visibility and promotion, in-event game visibility, and marketing materials permissions, please visit the Steam Themed Sale Events parent documentation page.

Eligibility

Games must be launched or in Coming Soon mode to be eligible for these events. If your game is not yet in Coming Soon, you can appeal to have your game join once you've released your Store Page. The base game must be eligible for the event; DLCs alone are not eligible.

To be eligible for this Fest, your Deckbuilding game must include the ability to both add and remove cards. The way you add/remove cards is up to you—random chance, after every boss fight, what have you—but you must be able to modify your deck of cards by progressing through the game.

Now, your game can have other elements in it that aren't just gaining/playing/destroying cards, but the deckbuilding portion better be a significant part of the game. In fact, if it's not called out in the store page in some way, it's probably not a good fit! A good minigame is always fun, but we want the deckbuilding to be a big part of it.

Another word on eligibility: while most TCG will be a good fit for this event, not all board games qualify. If you do manage your cards in the game, great! But if you just draw them, and have no way of managing them and can only play them in a specific order, this is not the event for you.

Games likely to be eligible will (at minimum) include one or more of these tags:
  • Deckbuilding
  • Roguelike Deckbuilder

Games likely to be eligible typically feature mechanics and/or elements including:
  • The game must involve acquiring and managing a deck of cards
  • The management of the deck does not necessarily have to be direct, games where you can influence the deck via events (eg Slay the Spire) are eligible
  • It doesn't necessarily have to be cards, but if it could not be communicated with cards, then it's probably not a good fit

Examples of games that may not include deckbuilding (as described above) as the primary game mechanic and thus may not be eligible include:
  • Games that feature 'cards' in deciding upgrades, and have no way of managing said cards

Check your list of eligible games here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/deckbuilders

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. I haven't received an email invitation and have a game I believe fits the eligibility criteria described above. How can I confirm whether my game has been invited and request an invitation if it has not been invited?
A. If you haven't received an email invitation and believe you have a game that fits the eligibility criteria described above, please check:

  • Your list of eligible games here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/deckbuilders
  • The registration page for your game by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/deckbuilders/<appid>
  • Your base game's App Admin page (in the Marketing Tools & Data section) for the opt-in by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/apps/landing/<appID>

If your game is not found via the paths above and you believe it meets the eligibility criteria, please use the appeals process available at the bottom of the registration page.

If your game was found via the paths above but you didn't receive an email, please check your Steamworks communication preferences (instructions available in the Steam Themed Sale Events parent documentation page).

Q. Do I need to register my game before entering a discount?
A. Yes, you need to do the following things in order:
  1. Follow the link in the invitation email, or click this link to see your eligible games and register each: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/deckbuilders
  2. Once a game is registered, you can then enter discounts on the Discount Management page: https://partner.steamgames.com/promotion/discounts/dashboard/

Q. Will there be livestreaming as part of this event?
A. Livestreaming will not be featured on the sale page, but you're welcome to livestream on your own game's store page as you wish.

Q. I've changed my plans. How do I cancel my registration?
A. Just return to the registration page for your game by inserting your appID at the end of this URL https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/deckbuilders/<appid> and deselect the checkbox at the very bottom of the page to remove your game from the event.
