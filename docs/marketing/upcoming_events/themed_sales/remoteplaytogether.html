Remote Play Together (2024)



Steam Remote Play Together Fest celebrates playing your favorite games with your friends, remotely!

This event runs February 12 at 10:00 AM PST (UTC-8) to February 19 at 10:00 AM PST (UTC-8) and will include eligible titles with sections for discounted titles, unreleased titles in Coming Soon mode, games with demos, and more.


For general information about Steam themed sale events, including details on the selection and invitation process, ways to participate (discounts, demos, and upcoming releases), event visibility and promotion, in-event game visibility, and marketing materials permissions, please visit the Steam Themed Sale Events parent documentation page.

Important Dates

  • February 8 - The trailer announcing the event launches on this day. At this point, you're welcome to share your participation in the event. Assets for this purpose will be available in the Asset Kit below.
  • February 12, 10am PST - The event starts. This is the deadline for entering a discount, and opting out if you no longer want to participate.

Eligibility

Games must be launched or in Coming Soon mode to be eligible for these events. If your game is not yet in Coming Soon, you can appeal to have your game join once you've released your Store Page. The base game must be eligible for the event; DLCs alone are not eligible. Eligibility is determined by the tags applied to your game's store page, as well as the gameplay that is evident from how you describe and represent your game on your store page.

Any game that supports the Remote Play Together feature, and has enabled the "Remote Play Together" feature on the game's store page, is eligible to participate.

Check your list of eligible games here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/remoteplaytogether_2024

Adding Remote Play Together Feature Support

If your title has local multiplayer support, it may be a good candidate for the Remote Play Together feature. The feature enables a player to invite a friend to join their game session via Remote Play streaming, even when their friend doesnâ€™t own the game, or even have a Steam account, as though they invited their friend over to their house. Remote Play Together can be a great way to introduce new players to your title.

The feature is enabled automatically for games that are indicated to have Local Multiplayer, Local Co-op, or Shared/Split Screen capability, but you can enable or disable it yourself to match whether this feature is appropriate for your game.

To enable the feature, edit your title's settings in the Steamworks Store Page Admin. On the Basic Info tab, under Supported Features, check Remote Play Together.

For more details, please see the Remote Play Documentation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. I haven't received an email invitation and have a game I believe fits the eligibility criteria described above. How can I confirm whether my game has been invited and request an invitation if it has not been invited?
A. If you haven't received an email invitation and believe you have a game that fits the eligibility criteria described above, please check:

  • Your list of eligible games here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/remoteplaytogether_2024
  • The registration page for your game by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/remoteplaytogether_2024/<appid>
  • Your base game's App Admin page (in the Marketing Tools & Data section) for the opt-in by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/apps/landing/<appID>

If your game is not found via the paths above and you believe it meets the eligibility criteria, please use the appeals process available at the bottom of the registration page.

If your game was found via the paths above but you didn't receive an email, please check your Steamworks communication preferences (instructions available in the Steam Themed Sale Events parent documentation page).

Q. Do I need to register my game before entering a discount?
A. Yes, you need to do the following things in order:
  1. Follow the link in the invitation email, or click this link to see your eligible games and register each: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/remoteplaytogether_2024
  2. Once a game is registered, you can then enter discounts on the Discount Management page: https://partner.steamgames.com/promotion/discounts/dashboard/

Q. Will there be livestreaming as part of this event?
A. Livestreaming will not be featured on the sale page, but you're welcome to livestream on your own game's store page as you wish.

Q. I've changed my plans. How do I cancel my registration?
A. Just return to the registration page for your game by inserting your appID at the end of this URL https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/remoteplaytogether_2024/<appid> and deselect the checkbox at the very bottom of the page to remove your game from the event.
