Steam VR Fest (2022)

Steam VR Fest celebrates the immersive world of virtual reality.

This event runs July 18 at 10:00 AM PDT (GMT-7) to July 25 at 10:00 AM PDT (GMT-7) and will include discounting, as well as sections for unreleased games and games with demos.

Eligibility

Games must have virtual reality capability in order to participate.

Invitation Process

We'll be inviting specific games to participate in Steam VR Fest based on the eligibility criteria above. This invitation will go out to Steamworks users who have set "Receive Steamworks Communication" permission for eligible games.

The invitation will ask you to register that game for Steam VR Fest, which is as easy as clicking one checkbox and saving. That registration will tell us which games intend to participate.

Once registered, users in your account with "Edit Pricing Permissions" will then see the Steam VR Fest sale appear in the new discounting approval tools and you can enter discounts for your game.

If you have not received an email invitation and believe that you have a game that fits the criteria described above, please check your list of eligible games. If your game is not present there and you believe it meets the above criteria, please contact us here.

Ways To Participate

Discounts

A discount is not required to participate in Steam VR Fest, though the Steam VR Fest page will most prominently feature discounted games. All invited titles can feature a discount unless you already have another discount scheduled too soon prior or following Steam VR Fest.

Reminder: As of March 28, 2022 all sales and discount opportunities are subject to the same 28-day cooldown between discounts. The only exceptions are the four major Steam seasonal sales. For more details on the discounting rule changes, please see Discounting Rule Changes.

Once invited, visit your discounting dashboard (Apps & Packages -> Sale Approvals) to see your invited games and to enter any discounts.

Enter your discounts any time before the sale begins on July 18 at 10:00 AM PDT.

Demos

If you've got an upcoming game that fits the criteria above (or even if you have a released game) you may release a demo to include in the fest. We'll have a section for games with free demos, as well as lists of upcoming releases.

Steam VR Fest Visibility

This is an official Steam Themed Sale and will be promoted significantly on the homepage of Steam and via our social media channels. The event will highlight games with discounts and will have sections for upcoming games and games with playable demos.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Do I need to register my game before entering a discount?
A. Yes, you need to do the following things in order:
  1. Follow the link in the invitation email, or click this link to see your eligible games and register each: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/vr_2022
  2. Once a game is registered, you can then enter discounts on the new Sale Approvals page here: https://partner.steamgames.com/promotion/discounts/dashboard/

Q. Will there be livestreaming as part of this event?
A. Livestreaming will not be featured on the sale page, but you are welcome to livestream on your own game's store page(s) as you wish. Note that because of the way the SteamVR.exe service interacts with broadcasting, you will need to use an external tool (such as OBS). That will give you the necessary control to configure your broadcast and let you point Steam directly at your game appID rather than SteamVR.exe. For more details, please see Setting Up A Stream.
